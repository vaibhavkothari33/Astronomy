    Our Vision

    Welcome to the Astronomy Club at Bennett University! We are a group of passionate stargazers, curious minds, and space enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the wonders of the universe. Our club provides a platform for students to learn about celestial phenomena, engage in discussions, and participate in exciting activities like telescope viewing, night sky observation sessions, and space-themed events. Whether you're an experienced astronomer or just starting to look up at the stars, our club offers a welcoming environment to share knowledge and foster a love for the cosmos.
