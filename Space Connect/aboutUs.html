    Our Vision

    They say reach for the stars, we say "Conquer the Cosmos" The Astronomy club at Bennett University aims for skyrocketing higher than the celestia as we conduct astronomical events on campus that keep Bennetians informed, updated and engaged in the cosmic events with exciting games and activities including astronomy themed movie nights where we screen "stellar" movies for the students, celestial debates and discussions, space themed scavenger hunts, and astrophotography events that keep the students engaged in the cosmos and makes them want to learn more. Join us if you have the spark to fire up your engine and let's roam to the endless supercluster of opportunities together.
