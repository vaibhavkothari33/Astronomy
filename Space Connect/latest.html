    Privatization of space & More

    The Astronomy Club at Bennett University organized a fruitful interactive session titled “Privatization of Space & More” featuring Dr. Vinod Kumar, Director of IN-SPACe. The event highlighted the growing role of the private sector in space exploration, fostering engaging discussions on innovation, regulation, and future opportunities. Dr. Kumar’s insights captivated attendees, inspiring curiosity and deeper exploration of the space industry. The successful event promoted knowledge-sharing and encouraged participants to explore careers in this dynamic field

    Astrophotography 2.0

    The workshop, held on December 2, 2023, was a highly enriching experience for participants. Led by expert astrophotographer Mr. Virendra Adhikari, the event offered in-depth lectures on advanced astrophotography techniques followed by hands-on training. Participants gained valuable insights into capturing deep-sky objects, image processing, and camera settings, with personalized guidance during the practical session. The collaborative atmosphere fostered learning, making the workshop both informative and inspiring for all attendees.

    Movie night 23'

    In an exciting collaboration with the Sunset Cinema Club, on 17th November 2023, at Bennett University, we hosted a movie night screening Interstellar, Men in Black (1997), and Across the Spider-Verse in three lecture halls. The event provided a relaxing break for students returning from the Diwali vacation. The screenings began at 9:30 p.m., with enthusiastic crowds filling the halls as club members addressed the audience. The night was marked by laughter, awe, and a range of emotions as students enjoyed the cosmic and cinematic experience.

    Cosmic Sabotage

    The Astronomy Club BU hosted Cosmic Sabotage, an event filled with an engaging and fascinating cosmic-themed mini-games session that turned the event into an extraordinary one. The participants entered two rooms and immediately felt enthusiasm coursing through their veins because of the cosmic atmosphere created by the club. Games and challenges like cosmic twist on beer pong, navigating obstacles and building pyramids were the highlight of the event. The cosmic-themed refreshment made the event more exuberant.
